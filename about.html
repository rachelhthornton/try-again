About

Welcome to our site, we're so excited you're here! We are a family owned business, a brother and sister duo with complimentary sets of expertise and a shared passion for bringing joy back into education.

We Believe ... in restoring student confidence. We work to build academic pride by challenging ourselves, valuing mistakes, and learning to grow from that process!

Our Approach ... We work closely with students one on one and in small groups, allowing us to provide personalized learning that meets them where they are. We will take the time to get to know and understand student needs. When we design a learning plan, we'll provide the background they're missing, update study strategies for their learning style, and encourage personal growth throughout.
