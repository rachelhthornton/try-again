Our Services

We offer remote (anywhere) or in person tutoring in Chapel Hill and Durham area (libraries) at a rate of $50/hr for one-on-one tutoring or pair tutoring.

Included in All Services For pre college students, we'd like discuss learning needs prior to the first meeting and will update parents after meetings (email available). For college students, our hours are more flexible during exam weeks, with remote meetings starting as late as 8PM. All students will have access to tutor contact information (email and phone), for scheduling, short questions, and links for additional resources.

The information we provide for parents, the flexibility of our hours, and our availability for student emails and texts outside the meetings are not an extra expense.
