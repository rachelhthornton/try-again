Scheduling

To get started, send us an email by copying this email address. Include all your questions, a brief description of your tutoring needs, and, when available, the course syllabus. For meeting requests, share your prefered days and times within a 10AM-7PM EST window. We check our email regularly and we'll match you with the best tutor.

Payments are made directly to your tutor and after each meeting.

For cancellations, including rescheduling, notify your tutor by 9PM EST the night before. They need time to plan their day! Cancellations made after 9PM EST the night before will still owe the allotted time's pay. Any overdue payments may result in scheduling delays.

Procrastinated? We've been there. Let us know if your meeting request is urgent - we will do our best to help with same-day requests.
